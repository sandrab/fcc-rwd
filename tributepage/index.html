Ruth van Klaveren Tribute

Who is Ruth van Klaveren?

Ruth van Klaveren is a great being. Although not a human being, still a very being being. She is friendly, amazing, soft-skinned and fluffy and she never swears.

Through the years she met many people and an occasional other cat being. She is happy with life as it is, so it seems.

Curriculum Vitae

  • May 4th 2005: Ruth gets born.
  • 2005 - 2008: Early years.
  • 2009-2015: Adult life.
  • October 15th 2015: Moving to a new home.

Hobbies and interests

Apart from sleeping and lying under the table for hours, Ruth really likes to run around the hallway like crazy sometimes. Other hobbies include yelling when dinner is not served on time.
